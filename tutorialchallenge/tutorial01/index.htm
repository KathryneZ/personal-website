About Me

Hi, I’m Kathryne, and I’m a double major in Sociology and Communications & New Media (CNM). Building this has been a blend of creativity and learning to me, and it’s given me a sense of ownership over something I’ve made from scratch (to some extent).

I like figuring out how people interact with design: what draws them in, what keeps them engaged, and what makes them quietly back away (hopefully not from this page). This site is a small collection of things I’ve built along the way. Feel free to explore~
